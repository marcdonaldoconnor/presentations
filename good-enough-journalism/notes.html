“All of these problems can be explained with one simple realisation…”

“… advertising triumphs all. News.com.au is ultimately a for-profit company, and that means that they are eventually beholden to the whims of the stakeholders, and the advertisers.”

  • Falls under Noam Chompsky’s model of manufacturing consent
  • Headlines are clickbait-y to encourage more people to click
  • It’s quicker to simply repeat what other people say rather than make your own analysis
  • Immigration is included to cater to their reader base, which is more right wing, in the hopes of building a loyal, rather than informed audience
  • It’s nothing more, nothing less

“Kohler doesn’t have to build a loyal audience through agreement, he builds an informed audience that trusts him as a reputable news source, which is far better for the public than news.com.au’s echo chambers.”

  • Kohler believes in an active audience, who votes with their feet
  • Always attempts to leave them informed
